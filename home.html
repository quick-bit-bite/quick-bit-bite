About Global Pulse

Global Pulse is a platform that connects people worldwide by capturing their daily mood in a 30‑second video. Whether you're in Times Square or a remote village, your video helps create a vibrant, global map of human emotion.

We believe every individual’s moment matters. Our mission is to showcase authentic, personal experiences through simple, user‑generated video clips, all without relying on heavy AI processing. Instead, we use standard web technologies to bring people together in a creative and engaging way.

Join us in celebrating the diverse emotions that shape our world, and explore the ever‑changing pulse of humanity.
